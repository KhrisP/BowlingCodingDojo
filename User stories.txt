1-	The game consists of 10 frames.  In each frame the player has two opportunities to knock down 10 pins.  
	The score for the frame is the total number of pins knocked down
	
2- a game consists of 10 frames. The score is the total of the frames' score

3- A spare is when all the 10 pins are down in two tries.  The bonus is the score done by the next roll.   

4- A strike is when all the pins are down a the first try. The bonus is the score done by the 2 next rolls

5- In the 10th frame, if a spare or a strike is done, an extra roll is given to the player. The last frame may content 3 rolls.

6- The maximum score is 300.
